About



Started in 2001, Penny Juice of America has been providing quality juice concentrates to
childcare facilities and preschools across the country. We are a family-owned and operated
business with over 40 years of experience in the beverage industry. Our goal is to provide
healthy high-quality juices at an affordable price, with flavors children will love!


All our products meet the requirements of the FDA with regard to subsidy and nutrition.
